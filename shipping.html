Shipping and Delivery Policy

Order Processing & Shipping Timeline

Orders are processed within 2–3 business days after confirmation. Delivery times may vary by destination but typically range from 5–10 business days after shipment.

Shipping Costs & Surcharges

  • GST as applicable will be charged extra.
  • Fuel surcharge: 20% for Exclusive services.
  • Single piece above 30kg: Rs 5/- per kg handling charge applies.
  • FOV: 0.2% on total invoice value.
  • Booking for Leh Ladakh: Charged as "ROI" FASTRACK only.
  • Volumetric or dimensional weight: Used if greater than actual weight; calculated as (L x B x H in cm) / 5000.

Shipping and surcharges outlined above will be calculated and communicated at the time of booking or checkout. Some destinations and services may attract additional charges.

Additional Information

  • No carriage of prohibited or restricted items (see Terms & Conditions).
  • Contact our support team for any questions regarding shipping cost calculations.
