Return and Exchange Window

Customers can return or exchange products within 30 days from the purchase date. Returns or exchanges requested after 30 days will not be accepted.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team by email at: returns@example.com. Please include your order number and reason for the return or exchange.

Refund Processing Time

Once your return or exchange request is approved and we receive the returned product, refunds will be processed within 15 business days. You will be notified via email once the refund has been issued.
